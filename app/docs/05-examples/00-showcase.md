## Themes in Action

### Minimal
Clean, professional documentation
- Best for: APIs, enterprise docs
- Colors: Blue, white

### Pixel
Retro gaming aesthetic
- Best for: Game dev, fun projects
- Colors: Green on dark

### Glass
Modern glassmorphism
- Best for: Premium products
- Colors: Purple, transparent

### Cyber
Neon cyberpunk
- Best for: Tech, sci-fi
- Colors: Cyan, magenta

### Terminal
Command-line vibe
- Best for: Developer tools
- Colors: Green on black

### Halloween
Spooky seasonal
- Best for: Fun, creative
- Colors: Orange, purple

### Synthwave
Retro future
- Best for: Creative portfolios
- Colors: Pink, sunset
